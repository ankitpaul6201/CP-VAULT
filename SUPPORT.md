# Support

Thank you for using CP Vault! Here are the best ways to get help.

---

## 🐛 Found a Bug?

Open a [Bug Report](https://github.com/ankitpaul6201/CP-VAULT/issues/new?template=bug_report.md).

Please include:
- Your browser and version
- Extension version (from `chrome://extensions/`)
- Platform where the bug occurred
- Steps to reproduce
- Console logs (F12 → Console)

---

## 💡 Have a Feature Idea?

Open a [Feature Request](https://github.com/ankitpaul6201/CP-VAULT/issues/new?template=feature_request.md).

---

## ❓ General Questions

Check the [FAQ](./FAQ.md) first — your question may already be answered.

For discussions, use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/ankitpaul6201/CP-VAULT/discussions).

---

## 🔒 Security Issue?

Do **not** open a public issue. See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for responsible disclosure.
